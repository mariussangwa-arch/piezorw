Our Mission

Innovation

We aim to bring creativity to renewable energy generation through smart footstep systems.

Community Impact

Improving everyday life by providing affordable, sustainable energy to Rwandan communities.

Education

Inspiring students and young engineers to contribute to sustainable development goals (SDGs).

Technology Behind the Project

Piezoelectric Sensors

Converts mechanical pressure from footsteps into electric voltage using special crystals.

Energy Storage

Collected energy is stored in capacitors and used to power LED street lights efficiently.

Smart Integration

IoT integration allows live tracking of generated power and energy analytics.
